Riders

How a ride works

Three beats — the same ones you'll feel in the app.

Pick where you're going

Search an address, choose a saved place, or drop a pin. Pickup and destination stay visible before you commit.

Confirm and match

Choose your ride option and payment method. We connect you with an available driver nearby.

Track and finish

Watch the map as your driver arrives, ride with live updates, then wrap up with an in-app receipt.

Help

Quick answers

More topics in our full FAQ — or email us anytime.

How do I pay for rides?

Add a payment method when you confirm a trip. Nisu supports card payments; charges are processed when your ride ends.

Is my location shared?

Location is used to match you with drivers, show progress during a trip, and power navigation. We don't sell your location. Details are in our Privacy Policy

How do I get help?

Email contact@nisu.info for account, ride, or payment questions — we read every message.
