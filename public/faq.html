Help & frequently asked questions

Last updated: February 2025

How do I book a ride?

Enter your pickup and destination in the search fields, or pin locations on the map. Select your ride type and confirm. Available drivers will be shown; once a driver accepts, you can track their arrival and your ride in real time.

How do I pay for rides?

Add a payment method in the ride confirmation flow. Nisu supports card payments. Payment is processed at the end of your ride.

Can I cancel a ride?

Yes. You can cancel before the driver arrives. Cancellation fees may apply depending on timing. Check the ride screen for cancellation options.

How do I become a driver?

Download the Nisu Driver app and complete registration. You will need to provide required documents and pass verification. Approval is subject to Nisu's driver requirements.

Is my location shared?

Your location is used to match you with nearby drivers, show your position during a ride, and for navigation. We do not share your location with third parties except as necessary to provide the service (for example, with your driver during an active ride). See our Privacy Policy for details.

Where is Nisu available?

Nisu is built for Kosovo and may be available in nearby regions as we expand. The app will show whether service is offered in your area when you open it.

What if something goes wrong during a trip?

Your safety matters. Use in-app tools where available, and contact contact@nisu.info with ride details. For emergencies, contact local emergency services first.

Does Nisu employ drivers or own cars?

No. Nisu is a technology platform that connects riders and drivers. Drivers are independent users of the service, not employees of Nisu. See our Terms of Service for the legal overview.

How do I contact support?

Email us at contact@nisu.info for help with your account, rides, payments, or any other questions.
