Our story

Mobility, made quietly excellent.

Nisu exists to move mobility forward — not with gimmicks, but with software that respects attention: fast where it matters, calm everywhere else, and honest about what we do (and don’t) provide.

What we believe

Principles that guide design, operations, and how we talk to riders, drivers, and partners.

Clarity beats spectacle

Interfaces should disappear into the task — booking, driving, supporting — not compete with it.

Trust is engineered

Safety tooling, transparent pricing surfaces, and reachable support aren’t “nice-to-haves.” They’re core product.

Local context matters

Real streets, commuting rhythms, and local commerce shape how Nisu should feel — not generic templates from elsewhere.

What Nisu is — and isn’t

We build technology

Nisu connects riders with independent drivers through software — routing, matching, payments tooling, and communications layers.

We don’t operate rides

Drivers are not Nisu employees; transportation is provided by drivers themselves. See our Terms for the legal framing riders and drivers agree to.

Where we operate

We expand thoughtfully, city by city. The app always reflects where rides are available today.

If we wouldn’t be proud showing a screen in an Apple keynote hallway conversation, we’re not done polishing it.
